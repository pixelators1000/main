About Our Operation

Where We Operate

Pixelators classes, events, and hackathons take place in libraries and community centers throughout the Bay Area. We believe in giving back to the community, so all of our events are organized with help from numerous libraries and community centers.

Pixelator's main operations, such as creating new material, planning events, etc. happen at our team members' individual households. To cut costs and avoid hassle, main operations do not happen at offices or conference rooms; instead, most communication is done through Slack and Google Hangouts, with several meetings between the leadership team. Pixelator's leadership team and numerous instructors frequently meet with library and city staff to plan new classes and organize new events.

None of Pixelator's locations are paid or run by businesses; all of our locations are community-based. Library and community center rooms have all the essential supplies: projector, whiteboard, tables, power cords, and internet connection. We believe that free, effective rooming is vital to the quality of a student's learning environment.

Our Staff and Leadership

The Pixelators staff and leadership team consists of middle and high school volunteers. None of our members are paid. We believe that a volunteer-based model fosters a sense of community and camaraderie among our members.

Pixelators volunteers consist of two main categories: facilitators and instructors. Facilitators help with organizing and running events, while instructors teach or help to teach students in our classes. All volunteers get documented volunteering hours as credit for their work.

Our syllabus, course content, and major operations such as social media, organizing major events, website design, and ideas for growth are all done by the leadership team. The leadership team works hard to give students and event participants a memorable and beneficial experience. Instructors also take on some leadership responsibilities when running their own branch, planning and organizing their event. Our staff and leadership are all intelligent and proficient in programming, eager to impart their knowledge to the next generation of kids.
